When AI can scaffold out entire features in seconds and you have multiple agents all working in parallel on different tasks, a ninety-second feedback loop kills your flow state completely.

We've recently invested in dramatically speeding up our developer feedback cycles, cutting some by 95% to address this. In this post we’ll share what that journey looked like, why we did it and what it taught us about building for the AI era.
